About EasyConnect!

EasyConnect est une plateforme multiservice en Tunisie. qui permet de mettre en relation des prestataires de services locaux avec des particuliers et des entreprises ayant des besoins spécifiques. EasyConnect offre une variété de services allant des services de nettoyage et de jardinage aux services de beauté et de bien-être, en passant par les services de santé et de travaux. L'objectif d'EasyConnect est de simplifier l'accès aux services locaux de qualité pour les particuliers et les entreprises en Tunisie tout en offrant aux prestataires de services une opportunité de développer leur activité et d'élargir leur portée grâce à une plateforme en ligne. EasyConnect se concentre également sur la création d'emplois et l'autonomisation des prestataires de services locaux.

Our Values

  • Quality: EasyConnect is committed to providing superior services to meet our customers' needs, and to helping local service providers grow their businesses through high quality standards.
  • Reliability: EasyConnect is committed to being a trusted platform for customers and local service providers, guaranteeing security, transparency and speed of transactions.
  • Accessibility: EasyConnect is committed to offering affordable services to enable all Tunisians to access the services they need.
  • Innovation: EasyConnect is committed to continuous innovation to provide an optimal user experience and solutions tailored to the needs of customers and local service providers.
  • Commitment: EasyConnect is committed to listening to its customers' needs and offering them responsive, high-quality customer service to answer their questions and solve their problems.

In short, EasyConnect's values are geared towards customer satisfaction, the development of local service providers and Tunisia's economic growth.
